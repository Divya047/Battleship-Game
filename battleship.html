Battleship Game

Instructions

Welcome to the Battleship Game! The objective of the game is to sink all of the computer's ships before the computer sinks all of your ships. You and the computer will take turns firing bombs at each other's boards. If you hit a ship, you will be able to fire again. If you miss, the computer will take its turn. The game ends when all of the computer's ships are sunk or all of your ships are sunk. Good luck!
