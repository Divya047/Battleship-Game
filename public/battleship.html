Battleship Game

Instructions

Welcome to the Battleship Game! The objective of the game is to sink all of the opponent's ships before the opponent sinks all of your ships. You and the opponent will take turns firing bombs at each other's boards. If you hit a ship, you will be able to fire again. If you miss, the opponent will take its turn. The game ends when all of the opponent's ships are sunk or all of your ships are sunk. Good luck!
